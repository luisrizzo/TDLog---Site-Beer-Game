Application in a Beer Game Simulation

Application in a Beer Game Simulation

The beergame is a simulation where players enact a four stage supply chain for a beer supply chain. Manufacturer, Distributor, Wholesaler and Retailer are the 4 roles played and each player has to decide everyday how much to order according to inventory levels and the demand it received. But what happens when humans are replaced by AI agents?

Learn more about the beer game

The goal is to produce and deliver units of beer: the factory produces and the other three stages deliver the beer units until it reaches the customer at the downstream end of the chain. The game is played in multiple Universities and MBA programs all around the World. You can play one online version of the game and compete agains other AI by clicking in image illustrating this comment. Can AI can beat classic Supply Chain Stock formulas?

Supply Chain formulas works with theoretical models and simplifications. But real world is doesn't behave that way and AI algorithms might achieve greater results by learning the nuances that are lost in the simplifications.
To test it we cannot stay on the theory, there need to be simulations and direct comparisons.

But how the AI learns?

How the AI learns?

Our AI is classed as using a Reinforced Learning Algorithm. After hundreds, thousands or even millions of simulations the AI algorithms learns how to achieve optimal total costs.But how?This is the same technique used by Deep Blue to beat Kasparov in Chess. Or more recently by Deep Mind to beat the human World Champion of Go.

Learning from interaction with the environment comes from our natural experiences. Reinforced Learning (RL) applies this concept to mathematical algorithms. To learn more click on the image
